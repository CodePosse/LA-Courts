Access to Courthouse Buildings

Jurors reporting for service November 9, 2024 may encounter various protest groups near some court buildings. Please plan extra time to navigate parking area and building entrances. Police and court security will be monitoring all activities to ensure entrance and egress to courthouses remain secure and unimpeded.
