 Mediation Volunteer Panel (MVP)

<Home > Mediation Volunteer Panel (MVP)
Unlimited civil cases referred by judicial officers to the Court's Mediation Volunteer Panel (MVP) are eligible for three hours of virtual mediation at no cost with a qualified mediator from the MVP. MVP mediators volunteer preparation time and three hours of mediation at no charge. If the parties agree to continue the mediation after three hours, the mediator may charge their market hourly rate. When a case is referred to the MVP, the Court's ADR Office will provide information and instructions to the parties. The Notice directs parties to meet and confer to select a mediator from the MVP or they may request that the ADR Office assign them a mediator. The plaintiff or plaintiff's attorney must submit an online MVP Response Form. The assigned MVP mediator will coordinate the mediation with the parties.
