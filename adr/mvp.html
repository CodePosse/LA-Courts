Mediaton Volunteer Panel (MVP)

Unlimited civil cases referred by judicial officers to the Court's Mediaton Volunteer Panel (MVP) are eligible for three hours of virtual mediaton at no cost with a qualified mediator from the MVP. MVP mediators volunteer preparaton tme and three hours of mediaton at no charge. If the parties agree to contnue the mediaton after three hours, the mediator may charge their market hourly rate. When a case is referred to the MVP, the Court's ADR Office will provide informaton and instructons to the parties. The Notce directs parties to meet and confer to select a mediator from the MVP or they may request that the ADR Office assign them a mediator.
