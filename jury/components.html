Please select one of the following reasons for your transfer. The law requires that jurors be randomly selected to the courthouse that has summoned them. You have been randomly selected to your courthouse. If you wish to move your service, you must request the change based on one of the legally sufficient reasons listed below. Place your cursor over each item and you will receive a detailed explanation of the reason.

Transfer Reason:

  1. My employer requires that I report to work before, during or immediately after jury service each workday. I must move my service to a courthouse closer to my work.
  2. I am a sole proprietor or I am a physician, lawyer, dentist, or in another profession which requires me to be on-call and available to my clients while on jury service. I must move to a courthouse closer to my business.

I live more than 20 miles from the courthouse that summoned me and moving my service to a closer courthouse would be less of a hardship.

I have a health/medical issue that requires me to be close to my home, my health care provider or health care facility.

I provide care for another person before and after jury duty or during the lunch hour and must be at a closer courthouse to provide this care.

I do not have a working vehicle and/or I do not drive and I must take three or more bus/public transportation transfers to arrive at the courthouse I am summoned to. A move to a closer courthouse would be less of a hardship.
