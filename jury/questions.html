Update Personal Info and Complete Questionnaire

Welcome Ramon Barnes! Complete the two forms linked below. When done, you will be automatically sent to Step 2 to help you prepare for jury duty.

1. I am a citizen of the United States.
2. I am able to read and understand English.
3. I am a resident of the County of Los Angeles.
4. I am atleast 18 years of age.
5. I am now serving as a grand or trial juror in any court of this state under a different JID number.
6. I have been convicted of malfeasance in office. (if No, skip question 6A below)
7. I am under a court-appointed conservatorship.
8. I am on active military duty and/or not domiciled in this state.
9. I am a peace officer appointed under PC §830.1, §830.15, §830.2(a), or §830.33(a).
10. I am currently incarcerated in prison or jail.
11. I have been convicted of a felony and am currently on parole. postrelease community supervision. felony probation, or mandated supervision for the conviction of a felony. I am required to register as a sex offender pursuant to Section 290 of the penal code for the conviction of a felony.
12. I am required to register as a sex offender pursuant to Section 290 of the penal code for the conviction of a felony.
