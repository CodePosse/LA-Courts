Know Before You Go

IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION For the duration of your jury service, do not report to the courthouse if you have symptoms of COVID19 (e.g., fever, cough, shortness of breath, body aches). Instead, please call the Jury Assembly Room phone number indicated on your summons form or (213) 972-0970 during court business hours and speak to court staff.

Be sure to follow the Los Angeles County Department of Public Health Guidelines for Isolation or Quarantine Guidelines as appropriate. Thank you.
