Make a plan to file your answer today.

You may only have 10 days until your landlord can ask the court to evict you without a hearing.

Respond Quickly
to avoid losing automatically

Do not ignore this. Your landlord might evict you and take owed rent from your paycheck or bank account. The judge will not hear your side if you don't act now.

  • Get a copy of the lawsuit. This paper is not the lawsuit. lf you don't have a copy of it, go to the clerk's office at the 1st floor of the Court and get a copy.
  • File an answer within 10 days. File Answer Form UD-105 by the deadline, even if you want to move out or work out a deal with your landlord.
