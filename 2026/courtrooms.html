PACE

The Professional Appointee Court Expenditure program (PACE) is a county program used to pay attorneys, doctors, investigators, experts, and other professionals appointed by the Superior Court of California, County of Los Angeles. ePACE is a web application that allows PACE claims to be submitted to the Court for payment.
