Types of Cases

Civil
A court system for private parties to resolve Disputes, Lawsuits, Evictions, Collections.
Criminal
Criminal offenses fall into one of three categories: Infractions, Misdemeanors, or Felonies.
Family Law
Child Custody, Child and Spousal Support, Divorce, Domestic Violence, Parentage, Restraining Orders.
Juvenile
For cases that involve children under 18 years old. Dependency Court and Juvenile Justice Court fall under this category.
Mental Health
A centralized court for cases involving mental disorders and mental health legal issues.
Probate
Descendent's estates, Trusts, Guardianship, Conservatorships and Minor's compromises
Small Claims
A civil court handling disputes of $12,500 or less.
Traffic
Involves a citation or ticket written by a law enforcement officer. Violations: Traffic laws and other non-traffic offenses.
Appellate
The Appellate Division reviews the trial court decisions, and all evidence presented.
